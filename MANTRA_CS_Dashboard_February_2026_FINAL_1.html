Executive summary
·148 total interactions logged in February across 4 platforms. Telegram dominated with 59%, followed by Twitter (18%), Discord (14%), and Email (8%).
·Ticker Change (23.6%) and Migration (18.2%) were the top query types, driven by community questions ahead of the OM → MANTRA chain upgrade on March 2. ·Discord FRT averaged 20 minutes across 9 ticketed interactions, with all categories well within benchmark thresholds.
·User activity peaked between UTC 06:00–08:00, aligning with morning hours across the Americas and Europe/MENA regions, which together accounted for 92% of interactions.
·Users were most active from the Americas (51%) and Europe/MENA (41%), with lower activity from Asia-Pacific (9%).
·Compared to January (165 total interactions), February saw an overall volume decrease of 10.3%, driven primarily by a drop in Telegram (108 → 88) and Discord (31 → 21). Email emerged as a new platform in February with 12 interactions.
